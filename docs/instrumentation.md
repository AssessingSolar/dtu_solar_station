#### Weather measurements

The following meteorological parameters are measured by a weather transmitter, type WXT520, from the Finnish company Vaisala Oyj:

* Air temperature
* Relative humidity
* Air pressure
* Wind speed
* Wind direction
* Rain intensity
* Rain duration
